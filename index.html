LAUNCH

Team One has a deep and long-standing history of launching the remarkable. We are powered by people with great ideas and we’re giving them the opportunity to apply that big thinking to impact a community in need. The top team will receive $25,000 to make their winning idea a reality, plus access to all the capabilities that our full-service agency provides.

THE ASSIGNMENT

Collaborate with T1 employees to launch an original idea that will benefit the community—not just you.

SUBMIT AN IDEA

Submit your team’s original idea by Friday, November 10, 2016. Convince us why we should invest in it without resorting to blackmail.

INITIAL JUDGING

T1 Leadership will vet the ideas for originality, potential for positive impact, and financial feasibility. The top ideas will be announced week of Tuesday, November 14, 2016.

ALL-AGENCY PITCH

The top teams will pitch their ideas to the entire agency during our All Agency Meeting week of November 14, 2016. Employees will vote and the winning team will be announced that day.
